Results

Overview

We are able to complete full games with minimal error from the robot, especially as the game progresses and the small deviations in piece positions accumulate. We were able to make our haptic search work efficiently and properly for most scenarios. As for our gameplay, we hit all edge cases as well as our circuit worked on time and provided enough suction and push throughout the entire game.

One minor problem we had is if the piece was drastically misaligned from the center of the square, the suction gripper would suck the board resulting in false positives.
